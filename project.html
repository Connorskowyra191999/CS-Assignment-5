Transportation and Emissions

This project attempts to find a relationship between the transportation industry and an increase of emissions. Using summaries and visualizations from R, we are attempting to see harmful contaminants are being removed from the atmosphere or not. The conclusion confirms we have been experienced decreased emission amounts from 2015 - 2022 due to political, and societal factors. To improve this project, more data regarding greenhouse gas emissions from the agriculture and utility industries would have been beneficial.
